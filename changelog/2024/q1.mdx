Q1 2024 is the earliest archived quarter in Camila's changelog. January through March established many of the foundational capabilities that the product continues to build upon — from multi-workspace support and public sharing links to the activity timeline that gives every record a full history.

### v1.4.0 — March 2024

- **Feature:** Recurring tasks — schedule records to repeat on daily, weekly, or monthly cadences, with options for end dates and repeat counts.
- **Feature:** Email-to-record — send an email to your workspace inbox address to automatically create a new record, with the email subject mapped to the record title and the body to the description field.
- **Improvement:** Notification preferences now support per-channel granularity, allowing users to independently configure email, in-app, and push notification settings for each event type.

### v1.3.0 — February 2024

- **Feature:** Public sharing links — generate a read-only shareable link for any record or view, accessible to anyone without requiring a Camila account.
- **Feature:** Activity timeline — a chronological log of all changes made to a record, displayed directly on the record detail page so teams can track the full history of edits and updates.
- **Improvement:** Color themes for workspaces — choose from 8 accent colors to visually distinguish between workspaces at a glance.

### v1.2.0 — January 2024

- **Feature:** Multi-workspace support — users can now belong to multiple workspaces and switch between them without logging out, with independent permissions per workspace.
- **Bug Fix:** Fixed incorrect sort order when combining multiple sort fields on list views, where secondary sort criteria were not being respected.
- **Improvement:** First meaningful paint time reduced to under 1 second on broadband connections, significantly improving perceived load speed for returning users.
