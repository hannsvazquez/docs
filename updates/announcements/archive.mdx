This archive contains every product announcement published by the Camila team, in reverse chronological order. Use it to find context on past launches or review the history of the platform.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Q2 2025 (April–June)">
    - **Bulk Export is live** — Export thousands of records at once in CSV or JSON. Built for teams that need to move their data into data warehouses, BI tools, or external workflows without manual effort.
    - **Advanced Dashboard Filtering** — Multi-condition filters, saved filter sets, and shareable filtered views give every team member the exact slice of data they need, when they need it.
    - **Real-time search indexing** — Search results now reflect changes instantly, so newly created or updated records show up in queries without any delay.
    - **Custom notification rules** — Take control of your inbox by defining exactly which events trigger notifications, for which record types, and delivered through which channels.
    - **API rate limit headers** — All API responses now include rate limit status headers, making it easier for developers and integrations to manage request volume and avoid interruptions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Q1 2025 (January–March)">
    - **Team Workspaces** — Invite your entire organization, assign roles, and collaborate in a shared environment. The biggest step forward in how teams work together inside Camila.
    - **Webhook signature verification** — Every outbound webhook now includes a cryptographic signature, so you can verify that events genuinely originate from Camila and build integrations with confidence.
    - **Dark mode** — A fully supported dark mode across the entire Camila interface, available in user preferences. Your eyes will thank you.
    - **Keyboard shortcuts** — Power users can now navigate and take action across Camila without touching the mouse, with a full set of customizable keyboard shortcuts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Q4 2024 (October–December)">
    - **Camila 2.0 launch** — Our most significant release ever. A unified API, native real-time collaboration, and a performance overhaul that sets the foundation for everything we're building next.
    - **Real-time collaboration** — Work alongside teammates simultaneously with live presence indicators, instant updates, and automatic conflict resolution across all record types.
    - **Saved views** — Create, name, and return to any filtered or sorted view of your data. Share views with teammates or keep them private — your workflow, your way.
    - **Audit log** — A complete, tamper-proof history of every action taken in your workspace, giving administrators full visibility for compliance and accountability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Q3 2024 (July–September)">
    - **Integrations marketplace launch** — A curated catalog of native integrations connecting Camila to the tools your team already relies on, with one-click setup and no engineering required.
    - **Custom fields** — Define your own field types and attach them to any record, so Camila fits the shape of your data rather than the other way around.
    - **Advanced search** — A new search experience with field-level filtering, boolean operators, and saved search queries for teams who need precision at scale.
    - **Data import** — Bring existing data into Camila from CSV files or connected sources, with field mapping, validation, and duplicate detection built in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Q2 2024 (April–June)">
    - **Two-factor authentication** — Protect your workspace with 2FA support for all users, available via authenticator app or SMS, with admin enforcement options for security-conscious teams.
    - **API key management** — Generate, rotate, and revoke API keys directly from the settings panel, with per-key permission scopes and usage monitoring.
    - **Reporting dashboard** — A dedicated analytics space with pre-built reports and custom chart support, giving decision-makers the metrics they need without leaving Camila.
    - **Comment threads** — Contextual conversations attached directly to any record, keeping discussion close to the work and reducing the need for external communication tools.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Q1 2024 (January–March)">
    - **Multi-workspace support** — Manage multiple distinct workspaces from a single Camila account — ideal for agencies, consultants, or enterprises operating across separate business units.
    - **Recurring tasks** — Set any task to repeat on a schedule, with flexible recurrence rules and automatic carry-forward for incomplete work.
    - **Public sharing links** — Share specific views or records with external stakeholders via a secure link, with no Camila account required to view.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
