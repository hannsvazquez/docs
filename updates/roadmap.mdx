The Camila roadmap gives you a forward-looking view of what we're building. Items move through three stages: **Planned**, **In Progress**, and **Shipped**. This page reflects our current thinking and is updated each quarter.

<Note>
  Roadmap items are subject to change. We share them to give you visibility and to gather feedback — not as firm commitments.
</Note>

## Now (In Progress)

These features are actively in development and expected to ship in the near term.

- **AI-assisted search** — Natural language queries across all record types, so you can find anything without knowing the exact field name or filter logic. *(Estimated: Q3 2025)*
- **Mobile app for iOS and Android** — Native apps with full offline support, keeping you productive whether you're in the office or on the go. *(Estimated: Q3 2025)*
- **Advanced permissions** — Field-level access control per role, giving administrators precise control over who can view or edit sensitive data. *(Estimated: Q3 2025)*

## Next (Planned)

These features are scoped and scheduled for upcoming quarters.

- **Data pipeline integrations** — Native connectors for Snowflake, BigQuery, and Redshift to bring your data warehouse into Camila without custom engineering. *(Q4 2025)*
- **White-label support** — Custom branding per workspace for enterprise customers, including logos, color schemes, and domain configuration. *(Q4 2025)*
- **SLA and uptime dashboard** — Real-time reliability stats visible directly in-app, so your team always knows platform health without leaving Camila. *(Q1 2026)*

## Recently Shipped

These items have crossed the finish line and are available to all users today.

- [Real-time collaboration](/changelog/2024/q4) — Work alongside your team simultaneously, with live cursors, instant updates, and no more version conflicts.
- [Team workspaces](/changelog/2025/q1) — Invite your whole organization, assign roles, and centralize your work in one shared environment.
- [Advanced dashboard filtering](/changelog/2025/q2) — Slice and dice your data with multi-condition filters, saved filter sets, and shareable filtered views.
- [Bulk export](/changelog/2025/q2) — Export thousands of records at once in CSV or JSON, on your schedule.
