Whether you have a support question, want to report a bug, or have feedback on the roadmap, the Camila team is here to help. Use the options below to reach the right person.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Support" icon="life-ring">
    Email [support@camila.app](mailto:support@camila.app) for technical help, billing questions, and account issues. Please include your **workspace ID** in the subject line so we can pull up your account immediately.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Bug Reports" icon="bug">
    Use the in-app **"Send Feedback"** button for the fastest response, or email [bugs@camila.app](mailto:bugs@camila.app). Please include clear steps to reproduce the issue, your workspace ID, and what you expected to happen versus what actually occurred.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Feature Requests" icon="lightbulb">
    Share your ideas at [feedback.camila.app](https://feedback.camila.app). Browse ideas already submitted by the Camila community, upvote the ones that matter most to you, or submit your own. The product team reviews all submissions regularly.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Product Marketing" icon="envelope">
    For press inquiries, partnership opportunities, or co-marketing requests, email [marketing@camila.app](mailto:marketing@camila.app). Our team will get back to you within 2 business days.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Before You Contact Support

Save time by checking these resources first — many common issues are already answered or resolved.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Changelog">
    Review the [Changelog](/changelog/introduction) to see if your issue has already been fixed in a recent release. We ship patch releases regularly, and the fix you need may already be live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the FAQ">
    Visit the [FAQ](/updates/faq) to see if your question is answered. It covers the most common questions about releases, breaking changes, API versioning, and how to report issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Status Page">
    Visit [status.camila.app](https://status.camila.app) to check for any active incidents. If there's an ongoing outage or degradation affecting the feature you're using, it will be listed there with live updates from the engineering team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reach out with full context">
    If you've checked the above and still need help, contact us with your **workspace ID** and a clear description of the issue — including steps to reproduce, what you expected, and what actually happened. The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Support is available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM UTC. We aim to respond to all tickets within 1 business day.
</Note>
