## How to use this space

Product Marketing publishes updates when major or minor releases ship, when roadmap items move to "in progress" or "shipped", and when strategic announcements affect users. Whether you're a decision-maker tracking platform direction or a day-to-day user staying on top of new capabilities, this space is designed to give you the right level of context — focused on impact, not implementation.
