## How this site is organized

Camila's release documentation is split into two spaces to serve different audiences:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Changelog (Technical)">
    The **Changelog** tab contains detailed, engineer-facing release notes. Every entry documents the technical changes shipped — new capabilities, configuration changes, performance improvements, and resolved bugs. Entries are organized by quarter and tagged by type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product Updates (Marketing)">
    The **Product Updates** tab is maintained by the Camila Product Marketing team. It translates releases into product stories, highlights major milestones, and communicates what's coming on the roadmap — written for business users, decision-makers, and the broader Camila community.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay current">
    Bookmark this hub and check back after each release cycle. The most recent quarter is always pinned at the top of the Changelog, and the latest announcement is featured in Product Updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Looking for a specific fix or feature? Use the search bar at the top of the page to find any entry by keyword, version number, or release date.
</Note>
